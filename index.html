Overview
Discover Tata Carnatica: A Symphony of Lifestyle & Culture At Shettigere Main Road, Devanahalli, North Bangalore

Tata Carnatica is a premier, integrated township by Tata Housing, set to redefine luxury living in Devanahalli, North Bangalore. Spread across a sprawling 140-acre site—divided into ~100 acres of residential and ~40 acres of commercial space—it combines housing, retail, workspaces, and green zones seamlessly. Named after Karnataka’s Carnatic music tradition, the township is designed around a central “spine road” with diverse zones like Tata Swaram (11+ acres of plotted developments) and Tata Raagam
